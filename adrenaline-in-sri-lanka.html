Adrenaline in Sri Lanka

Locations Covered

Day 01

Arrival / Colombo

Now you have arrived in Sri Lanka for your ultimate adventure experience. We will be picking you up from the airport and transporting you to the commercial capital of the island, Colombo. Packed with exciting sights to see and beautiful beaches on the coast, this is the perfect place for you to wind down before starting your thrilling adventures.

Day 02

Colombo / Dambulla / Sigiriya

From Colombo, we will journey on to Dambulla. Dambulla, located in Sri Lanka's Central Province, is a treasure trove of ancient structures that are well worth a visit. The spectacular Dambulla cave temple complex should be at the top of your list of Dambulla attractions. Dambulla is also home to South Asia's largest rose quartz mountain and the RangiriDambulla International Stadium. The beautiful Sigiriya fort and the nearby Minneriya national park are well worth your time and attention. Choose the top Dambulla sites to visit and experience Sri Lanka like never before, from historic temples to panoramic vistas from sky high cliffs. It is a stunningly gorgeous city that will undoubtedly captivate visitors. When you have explored every inch and been fascinated by the Dambulla Cave Temple, we will then journey on to Sigiriya, the beautiful lion rock that stands to amaze all visitors to Sri Lanka.

Day 03

Sigiriya / Polonnaruwa

From Sigiriya, we will unleash the adventurer in you straight to Polonnaruwa. One can only imagine what an old city in Sri Lanka's Northern district will have in store for travelers from all walks of life. However, guess what? There are so many fun things to do in Polonnaruwa that you'll want to come back for more. That's true, from cultural attractions to historical places, adventurous, natural attractions to spiritual activities, the list is so broad that listing all of them in a single blog article may not do the local attractions in Polonnaruwa credit. This location is unquestionably a goldmine for photographers and bloggers! Following this lovely adventure, we will then return to Sigiriya to turn in for the day.

Day 04 & 05

Sigiriya / Knuckles

Hello and welcome to Knuckles, Sri Lanka. Misty mountains, flowing tea plantations, cascades of waterfalls, picture-perfect mountain communities, and a spectacular array of animal and plant species are just a few of the lovely mountain country's features. We make it our duty to provide you with a honest glimpse into our world that you will never forget. Knuckles impresses us with its uniqueness and shocks us with its numerous unexplored places. Whether you're climbing, hiking, trekking, looking for leisure in beautiful settings, or going on a discovery tour, the enthralling mountainous region will exceed all of your expectations. Join us on an unforgettable excursion into the still-unexplored mountain regions.

Day 06 & 07

Knuckles / Kithulgala

From Knuckles, we will take on the mantle to give you a life-changing experience at the white water rafting in Kithulgala. Explore the crystal-clear waters of Sandun Ella Waterfall, which is surrounded by stunning vistas of the hill region. Our knowledgeable tour guides are experts who pay close attention to client safety and utilize high-quality equipment that satisfies international requirements. The sight of the thunderous waterfall tumbling over its rocky valley will give you a burst of excitement. You don't need to be able to swim or have any prior abseiling expertise. We will supply you with all of the necessary safety guidelines. Have the opportunity to participate in a variety of other fascinating activities available in Kitulgala, such as canyoning, white water rafting, and treks through the jungle, caverns, and rubber plantations.

Day 08

Departure

Now that you have finished your adrenaline-filled rush through the beautiful scenes of Sri Lanka, it is now time for you to head back home. We hope that the country has captured your heart and that you will return for more unforgettable experiences.
